Facility Bookings

Our downtown location can be booked for suitable events, and have been configured for lectures, movie screenings, and dance parties. Rates vary depending on the nature of event and how much setup support you need.

Maximum occupancy is 48 people. Chairs, tables, a projection screen, and other useful equipment is available if needed.

Note: we have only one partly accessible washroom.

Please contact us to book our facility.

Lunch & Learn

You can book us for a 45-60 minute lunch-and-learn presentation for your office or group! We'll introduce key information for individuals interested in riding, including gear, on-road safety and basic mechanical checks.

This presentation can be tailored to a specific focus. For availability and to make a booking, please contact us and provide as much lead time as possible.

Rent-a-Mechanic

Book our certified bike mechanics! Available services include:
- tune-up stations at your workplace or event
- lunch-and-learn presentation on mechanics and riding
- instructional seminars at your workplace or event

For availability and pricing, please contact us.

Treaty Six Territory

Bike Edmonton acknowledges that we are located on Treaty 6 territory, a traditional gathering place for diverse Indigenous peoples including the Cree, Blackfoot, Métis, Nakota Sioux, Iroquois, Dene, Ojibway / Saulteaux / Anishinaabe, Inuit, and many others whose histories, languages, and cultures continue to influence our vibrant community. We acknowledge that we are all treaty people and we are committed to equity and justice for all our relations.
